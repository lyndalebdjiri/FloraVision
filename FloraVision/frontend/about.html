Why FloraVision Exists

Flowers have always been speaking.
We built a way to listen.

Every flower carries a name, a season, a history a quiet story about where it grows and what it needs. Most of us pass by without knowing it.

FloraVision closes that distance. Photograph any flower and, within seconds, its world opens to you. Not just for botanists, but for anyone who has ever paused mid-walk, curious about what caught their eye.

For the new gardener, the wandering explorer, the curious child for anyone who believes that knowing the name of something beautiful makes it even more beautiful.

Beauty, named and understood.
Knowing a flower's name
is the first step into its entire world.
